Hanauma Bay

Hanauma bay has a beautiful beach and has a lot of sea life. It is great for snorkeling and sun bathing.

Lanikai Beach

Lanikai Beach is one of Hawaii's most picturesque beaches. It has calm waters for snorkeling and kayaking.

image source





Kailua Beach

Kilua Beach was named "America's Best Beach" in 1998. It has miles of light colored sand and clear water. There is also a 35 acre park outfitted for many beach sports.

image source





Waimanalo Bay

Waimanalo bay has some rough waters great for boogie boarding. There is also some good fishing here. It is also one of the longest beaches on the island.

image source





Waikiki Beach

Waikiki beach is the most famous beach on the island. It is located close to downtown Honolulu. It has peaceful waters and there are a lot of attractions nearby.

image source





Sunset Beach

Sunset beach faces west thus prompting the name for wonderful evening views. It is located on the north shore where most of the biggest waves cana be found. Surfers love this beach.
