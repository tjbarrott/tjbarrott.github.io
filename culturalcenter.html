Get cultured

The polynesian cultural center has three main offers besides walking around the center enjoying the art, plants, and poynesian atmosphere.

  • Island villages
  • Luau and Dinner
  • Evening Show
The Island villages takes a tour of real Hawaiian villages where you can talk with natives.
The Luau and dinner is sure to fill you up. It also comes with authentic polynesian entertainment.
The Evening Show is featured in the picture with fire dancers. There are over 100 Polynesians performing during the show. It is sure to be entertaining.
